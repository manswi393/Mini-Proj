Smart Energy Meter

Smart Energy Meter is an intelligent web system that monitors electricity usage, tracks consumption patterns and predicts future power load using machine learning. The system helps users reduce electricity wastage, analyze daily energy usage and make smart decisions to save electricity and reduce cost.

About Our Mini Project

Electricity plays a vital role in modern life. Every home, industry and organization depends on electrical energy for daily operations. However, uncontrolled electricity usage leads to energy wastage, higher bills and environmental damage.

Our Smart Energy Meter project is developed to monitor electricity usage and provide intelligent prediction using machine learning technology.
